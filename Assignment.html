Dos are a domesticated descendant of wolfs, which are characterized by an upturning tail. The dog is derived from an ancient extinct wolf, and the modern wolf is the dog's nearest living relative. The dog is one of the first species to be domesticated by hunter gatherers over 15,000 years ago. Dogs have been selectively bred over the years for various behaviors, sensory capabilities and physical attributes. Nowdays there are around 450 dogs breeds that perform many rols for humans.

Dogs can have many positive effects on the lives of their owners. They influence social, emotional, and cognitive development in children, promote an active lifestyle, provide companionship, and have even been able to detect oncoming epileptic seizures or the presence of certain cancers.

Dog breeds

Dogs are the most variable mammal all over the world, there are around 450 breeds that are globally recognized. Modern breeds developed by human selection in the Victorian era, and since then, dogs have undergone changes by artifical selection imposed by humans. The difference between breeds is significant, and goes from morphology traitl like skull, body size, type of fur and color: to behavioral and personality traits, like their scent detection, guarding, agression and hunting habilities.
